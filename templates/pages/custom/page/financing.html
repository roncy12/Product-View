Apply For Financing

At King's Great Buys Plus, we greatly honor the needs of our customers. To meet our customer's needs, we offer a wide range of financing options: from well-known lenders like Wells Fargo to newer, more innovative lending companies such as American First Finance that provide shoppers a better yes so they can bring home their desired items even without a credit history plus the benefit of flexible payments.

When applying for financing at one of our store locations, we usually give feedback from the lender within a few minutes. Our customers can expect an immediate answer as to their acceptance for financing and the amount of their approved credit. If one application gets disproved, King's will then work with other lending sources to find a lender that will approve our customer.

This level of financial help isn't readily available in most stores. Here at King's however, It is our goal to offer the best shopping experience to our customers. and that includes helping them find ways to pay for the items they need. If you personally want to avail of this service, you can drop by any of our store locations anytime. Select the items that you want to purchase and allow us to help you find the financing option suited for your needs.

For any questions regarding financing, we recommend getting in touch with the nearest King's store in your area.
